Important Announcements

Major Changes to This Year's Contest

This year's contest will feature one individual student event and one school team event. Our individual event areas include MOS certification testing, Accounting, and General Business / Current Events. We have expanded the number of students each school can bring to the contest to six(6). There are also 10 slots open for teachers who want to receive Cosmo services during the contest; click the event below for more info.

Prizes for the Winning Team

Each school will participate in a team event. Each student (up to six [6]) on the winning team will receive a a Google Home Mini and one 3-credit hour tuition waiver to Lake Land College. Plus, one Google Home Mini will also be awarded to the instructor of the winning team.

What is MOS?

Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) certification is the leading IT certification in the world. More than 1 million MOS exams are taken every year in over 140 countries. Demonstrate you have the knowledge, skills, and abilities to productively use Microsoft Office. MOS enables you to tap the full features and functionality of the Microsoft Office system, resulting in heightened levels of individual performance, confidence, and differentiation.
